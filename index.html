Lasagna App

The Shopping Companion You've Been Waiting For

The Lasagna App by Cloud Front Studios creates a shopping list for you as you browse the internet. Forget about making a list, or having to make a second trip. Lasagna has your shopping list ready. Privacy and productivity are the essential ingredients that make up this app. Our self-contained web browser extension compiles a list of key topics as you browse the internet from a computer or laptop. Then from the convenience of your phone, a list of component pieces will already be on the list when you go shopping!
